Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller that follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets by entering people's dreams. He is given a final chance at redemption when he's tasked with planting an idea into someone's mind — a process known as inception.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page as Ariadne, Tom Hardy as Eames, and Ken Watanabe as Saito.
